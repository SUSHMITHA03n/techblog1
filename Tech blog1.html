Trends in Artificial Intelligence 2020

1) Deep learning: Deep learning is an Artificial Intelligence form, which develops algorithms called artificial neural networks working by modeling the human brain function and structure. Machine learning has been in demand for a long time, however, jobs including deep learning have risen 35 fold between 2015 and 2017. Numerous areas of Artificial intelligence technology like computer vision, autonomous vehicles, automatic text generation and so on are the fields where deep learning use is at its peak.

2)AI and Cloud computing AI and cloud computing are transforming business at every level. From deeper learning to near-compete automation of key processes, the potential is promising. While there are some examples of this in the market now, a look at the landscape suggests that this will only continue to grow in the years ahead. Begin to explore how AI and cloud computing together could help you deliver better experiences, work more efficiently, and capture the maximum value from the data and insights you collect in the market.
